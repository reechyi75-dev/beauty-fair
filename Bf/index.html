REQUEST GUIDELINES

When submitting a request for a Loan or a Day Off, please ensure you carefully read and understand the following guidelines:

Loan Application

  • Only staff who meet the required eligibility criteria will be considered.
  • A new loan will not be granted until the previous one has been fully repaid.
  • Misconduct, dishonesty, or failure to provide accurate information may result in automatic disqualification.
  • The approved loan amount may differ from the amount you requested.
  • Your guarantor must acknowledge and accept their responsibility before your loan can be processed.

Day-off Request

  • You are expected to resume work on or before the approved return date.
  • Staying beyond the approved period without notice may result in disciplinary action.
  • If unforeseen circumstances require you to extend your absence, you must inform your Admin or Supervisor immediately.

General Instruction

  • Be transparent and truthful when filling out any request form.
  • You may use the voice note option if you prefer.
  • Always uphold professionalism, respect workplace ethics, and strictly comply with company rules and policies both within and outside the premises.
  • All requests are subject to review and approval, and management reserves the right to decline any application that does not align with company standards or policies.

Our Mission

About This App

The Beauty Fair Staff App is designed to streamline communication, enhance collaboration, and improve efficiency across all departments. This comprehensive platform enables our team members to:

  • Stay connected with colleagues through direct messaging
  • Receive important announcements and updates instantly
  • Submit requests for loans and time off
  • Report issues and complaints confidentially
  • Share updates and celebrate achievements
  • Access HR resources and company policies

Teamwork

We believe in the power of collaboration and supporting one another.

Integrity

We maintain the highest standards of honesty and ethical conduct.

Innovation

We embrace change and continuously seek ways to improve.

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 2025

At Beauty Fair, we are committed to protecting your privacy and personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use the Beauty Fair Staff App.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

We collect the following information when you use the app:

  • Name and employee ID
  • Email address and phone number
  • Department and job role
  • Profile picture (optional)
  • Birthday and hobby (for profile)

Usage Information

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

We implement robust security measures to protect your personal information:

  • Encrypted data transmission using SSL/TLS protocols
  • Secure password storage with industry-standard encryption

  • Limited access to personal data by authorized personnel only

Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. Your data may be shared only in the following circumstances:

  • With HR and management for employment-related purposes
  • With other staff members as part of normal app functionality

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Opt-out of non-essential communications
  • Download a copy of your data

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as you remain an employee of Beauty Fair and for a reasonable period thereafter as required by law or for legitimate business purposes.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes through the app or via email.

Employee Guide

Welcome to Beauty Fair
Dear Ope,

We are pleased to have you join our team and we look forward to a successful working relationship with you. At Beauty Fair, we are committed to building a culture of integrity, teamwork, and excellence. This guide is designed to help you understand the policies, expectations, and opportunities that define your role within our organization.

Please take time to read through this guide carefully. It contains essential information about how Beauty Fair operates, the standards we uphold, and what is expected of every employee. Compliance with these guidelines is mandatory for all staff members.

1. Core Values

At Beauty Fair, we operate on the foundation of:

  • Integrity – conducting all activities honestly and ethically.
  • Respect – valuing colleagues, supervisors, and the organization as a whole.
  • Teamwork – working collectively towards shared goals.
  • Productivity – ensuring efficiency and high-quality performance.
2. Work Hours & Attendance
  • Official resumption time is 8:00 AM daily. Employees are expected to be at their duty posts promptly.
  • Break period is 1:00 PM – 1:50 PM (50 minutes).
  • Timeliness and consistent attendance are critical to maintaining efficiency.
3. Workplace Conduct

Employees are expected to:

  • Adhere strictly to all company policies and procedures.
  • Maintain professionalism at all times.
  • Avoid unnecessary movement during work hours, except during break.
  • Channel complaints or concerns through the Supervisor first before escalating to the Human Resources (HR) Department.
  • Always respect the authority of your Head of Department (HOD) and Supervisor.
Movement Policy: For security and order, all staff are required to use the back entrance when entering or exiting. The reception is not to be used by employees.
4. Discipline & Queries

Failure to comply with company policies may result in disciplinary action. This may include written queries depending on the severity of the misconduct.

Details on levels of queries and possible outcomes can be found on the Query Page within this application.

5. Security & Anti-Theft Policy
  • Theft in any form is strictly prohibited.
  • Any employee caught stealing will face immediate disciplinary measures, including termination and prosecution under the laws of the Federal Republic of Nigeria.
  • Employees who witness theft and fail to report it, either openly or through the anonymous reporting feature, will be considered accomplices and subject to the same consequences.
  • Security personnel are trained to monitor behavior closely and act decisively against misconduct.
6. Recognition & Rewards

Beauty Fair acknowledges and celebrates outstanding performance:

  • Each week, Supervisors nominate two staff members who have demonstrated exceptional commitment and performance.
  • Staff members vote to select the Staff of the Week.
  • An employee who wins Staff of the Week for four consecutive weeks becomes the Staff of the Month, which includes an additional salary benefit for that month.
This program reinforces our commitment to hard work, integrity, and excellence.
7. Employee Benefits

Beauty Fair provides opportunities for employee support through loans. Eligibility is based on:

  • Length of service with the company.
  • Demonstrated integrity and reliability.
  • Consistent performance levels.
  • Positive reports and recommendations from the employee's HOD.
8. Compliance Requirement

So long as you are within the company premises, you are required to abide by all policies and guidelines outlined in this Employee Guide. Non-compliance may result in disciplinary action, up to and including dismissal.

Closing Note

We believe that every employee plays an important role in the growth of Beauty Fair. By upholding our values and following the standards in this guide, you contribute directly to the success of our mission.

Once again, welcome to Beauty Fair. We are glad to have you on our team.

– Human Resources Department

Award

Everything You Need to Know About the Award Program

Kindly Read All Through

At Beauty Fair, we believe that hard work, commitment, and teamwork should never go unnoticed. Every member of our staff is a valuable part of the family, and the award program is our way of showing appreciation for the extra effort you put in each day.

Each week, the supervisor carefully observes everyone's performance and attitude at work. On Fridays, two outstanding staff members will be nominated based on their dedication, consistency, and positive impact. From there, the rest of the team will vote for who truly deserves to be named Staff of the Week.

Staff of the Week

Winning Staff of the Week is more than just a title — it is recognition of your effort, discipline, and contribution to the team. If a staff member earns this honor for four weeks in a row, they will automatically become the Staff of the Month. This achievement comes with a special company reward, which will be added to their salary as a token of appreciation.

Fair Voting System

At Beauty Fair, we value fairness, sincerity, and honesty. There is no room for bias in this process. When casting your votes, we encourage you to vote with truthfulness and recognize those who genuinely stand out.

This culture of recognition is not only about rewards, but also about building a supportive workplace where everyone is inspired to do their best. Every effort matters, and every small act of dedication brings us closer to shared success. Together, we grow, achieve, and celebrate as one team.
